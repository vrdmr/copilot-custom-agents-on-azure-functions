## Key Matchup Factors in Super Bowls

### What Wins Championships
1. **Quarterback play** — Elite QBs win 75%+ of Super Bowls
2. **Turnover margin** — Team with fewer turnovers wins ~70% of the time
3. **Run game** — Teams averaging 100+ rush yards win 65% of Super Bowls
4. **Defensive pressure** — 3+ sacks correlates with 68% win rate
5. **Red zone efficiency** — Scoring TDs vs FGs in the red zone is critical
6. **Coaching experience** — First-time Super Bowl coaches are 20-33 all-time

### Playoff Momentum vs Regular Season
- Wild Card teams have won 6 Super Bowls
- #1 seeds win ~30% of Super Bowls
- Bye week advantage is debatable — rust vs rest
